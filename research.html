Breeding programs grounded in science

Trimurti's R&D is led by Dr. Syamal Krishna Ghosh, Chief Innovation Officer — a Ph.D. in Genetics from IARI New Delhi with over 25 years in the seed industry. Our breeding programs are driven by career geneticists and seed technologists, not by short-term commercial targets.

We operate dedicated research centres positioned across India's major agro-climatic zones. Each centre focuses on developing hybrids and varieties tailored to the specific soil, water, and seasonal conditions of its region — because what works in Telangana may not work in Bihar.

Our current portfolio includes 120+ hybrids and varieties across field crops, vegetables, and fodder, with new products in the pipeline every season.

Breeding priorities

Our research programs are guided by the real challenges Indian farmers face in their fields every season.

Yield potential

Breeding for higher genetic yield potential under optimal and managed stress conditions across all crop segments.

Stress tolerance

Developing drought-tolerant, heat-tolerant, and water-logging tolerant hybrids to meet the challenges of climate variability.

Quality traits

Breeding for grain quality, oil content, nutrition profile, and consumer-preferred characteristics in every crop segment.
