About Us

Seed professionals, not just seed sellers

TriMurti Plant Sciences was founded in 2011 by seed industry professionals who believed that Indian agriculture deserved better — better genetics, better quality, and a better partnership model.

Image placeholder

A seed company built by seed people

TriMurti Plant Sciences Private Limited (TPSPL) was founded in 2011 by Raghu Vasu A, a Gold Medalist from IIM Kozhikode with deep roots in the seed industry. The company was built on a simple premise: that Indian farmers deserved access to superior genetics delivered with uncompromising quality.

From a focused beginning in field crops, TPSPL has grown into a full-spectrum seed company operating across the entire value chain — from parent-line breeding and hybrid development to processing, quality assurance, and a distribution network spanning India and Nepal.

Today, our seeds are cultivated on over 2.5 million acres annually, trusted by 200,000+ farmers and 5,000+ retailers nationwide.

Our mission & values

Every decision we make is guided by the same principle: deliver value to the farmer through science, quality, and integrity.

Science First

Our breeding programs are led by geneticists and seed technologists, guided by data and field performance — not by short-term commercial targets.

Quality Without Compromise

From seed production to processing and packaging, every lot is tested to meet our standards before it reaches the farmer.

Regional Relevance

One size does not fit all in Indian agriculture. Our R&D is positioned across agro-climatic zones to develop products for local conditions.

Partner-Centric

Our trade partners and dealers are not just distributors — they are our direct link to the farmer's field and a vital part of our quality loop.

Long-Term Horizon

Seed breeding is a long-cycle business. Our commitments to research, quality, and relationships are measured in years and decades, not quarters.

Farmer Success

Everything we do — every hybrid we develop, every seed lot we process — is ultimately measured by one metric: the farmer's harvest.

Our journey

Key milestones in TriMurti's evolution from a seed startup to a trusted name across India and Nepal.

2011

Founded in Hyderabad

TPSPL was incorporated with a focus on field crop hybrids for Indian growing conditions.

2013

First product launches

Initial hybrid maize and rice varieties introduced to market, receiving strong farmer adoption.

2016

R&D expansion

Established dedicated research centres across key agro-climatic zones to strengthen breeding programs.

2018

Nepal market entry

Expanded distribution network into Nepal, bringing TriMurti seeds to new geographies.

2019

Stanford Seed Program

Founder & CEO Raghu Vasu A selected for the Stanford Seed Transformation Program.

2021

120+ hybrids & varieties

Portfolio expanded to cover field crops, vegetables, and fodder — serving 2.5Mn+ acres annually.
